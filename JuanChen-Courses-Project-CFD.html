Microfluid is a kind of fluid in micron or nanometer scale. In the last ten years, with the rise of nanotechnology, the study of fluid has been extended from the macroscopic level to the microscopic level. The research needs on phenomena and properties of microfluid have shown an outbreak in many emerging application fields such as bioengineering, pharmaceutical, chemical and high-throughput device design and preparation, which has a direct effect on national economy and social development. Although the study of microfluid is the category of fluid research, its physical properties are different from traditional macroscopic fluid flow, and the theory and method of the later one are difficult to apply to microfluid. The traditional macrofluid is mainly based on the continuity assumption. With the narrowing of the scale, the effect of fulid motion properties of the classic Newtonian mechanics in traditional macrofulid on the movement of the fluid has decreased significantly, while the interaction between microscopic molecules and particles dominates. At this time, the traditional macroscopic continuous assumption is difficult to describe the microscopic movement properties of the fluid, and thereby can not capture the microscopic flow phenomena accurately. New research theories and methods of microfluid have become an urgent need for research in related fields.
The macro-micro coupling model is the key technology of microfluid simulation. It combines the macroscopic continuity hypothesis model and the microscopic molecular hypothesis model and has many advantages. From a computational point of view, it combines the molecular description and the continuous field description that are more computational efficient in the same computing model, which helps to make full use of the accuracy of microscopic simulation and the high efficiency of macroscopic simulation and achieve an accurate and efficient simulation of microfluidic phenomena.
Load balancing problem seriously affects the efficiency of large-scale parallel computing. CFD problem is solved based on the grid method. It is usually necessary to divide the data of simulation space based on the unit of grid. The computational grid allocated to each processor is fixed without considering the dynamic mesh. Therefore, the parallel division of the data directly determines the load balancing in the calculation process. For the MD simulation method, due to the relatively small number of communication coupling between processes, the optimization the parallel simulation is mainly focused on the load balancing problem in the simulation process. A processor corresponds to multiple microscopic particles, and the position of the particles is constantly changing in the calculation process, therefore the balance of computational load will change.

Background
Objective
1. Study the load balance problem of CFD/MD coupling method;
2. Read the literature of related fields;
3. Discuss and give research schemes;
4. Analyze the complexity of load balance problem in the coupling method compared to that in single scale method.
Tasks
1. Literature reading
Read the related literature, know more about the coupling simulation mechanism in which two scales CFD/MD are adopted, and analyze the load balance problem caused by coupling computation.
2. Case analyzing
By analyzing the straight pipe flow case that is simulated by CFD / MD coupling method, study the method that each time step advances forward in the iterative numerical solution process, and analyze the load balance problem caused by coupling computation.
3. Scheme designing
Design the load partition scheme for CFD/MD coupling simulation aimming at the computation of overlapped regions.
